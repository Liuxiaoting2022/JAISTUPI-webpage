Discover the Workshop

JAIST-UPI Student Academic Exchange Event

This workshop is jointly organized by the Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST) and Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI). It aims to provide a cross-border academic platform that fosters collaboration among students and encourages interactions between faculty members, enhancing the overall academic experience and strengthening ties between the two institutions.

Overview

The JAIST-UPI Student Academic Exchange Event is a unique platform that brings together students from Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST) and Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia (UPI) to exchange research ideas and foster academic collaboration. The event will feature presentations, discussions, and networking opportunities.
